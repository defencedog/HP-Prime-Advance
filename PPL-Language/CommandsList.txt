HP Prime Version 2.1.14181 (2018 10 16) commands-list sorted by groups in txt and xls format (V2.0)

Getting familiar to HP Prime I roughly sorted functions in "Command tree 13217.pdf" and "https://en.hpprime.club/docs/commands-list" into groups
  General
  Variables
  Drawing
  Geometry
  Statistics
  Probability
  Finance
  Solving
  Calculus
  Numbers
  Trigonometry
  Lists
  Matrix
  Polynomial
  Strings
  Bits
  Programs
Use mono-space font to display this file. Alternatively an excel version is available. Hope this file can simplify your way on Prime.

                                                                                                                        Frank P, 23 March 2019
